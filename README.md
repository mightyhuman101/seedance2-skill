## 📋 About seedance2-skill

seedance2-skill is a simple tool that helps you create better prompts to generate videos using seedance2.0. It guides you in writing clear and effective instructions to get the best results from the video generation software.

This skill is useful if you want to make videos from text prompts but don’t know how to write them well. With seedance2-skill, you can improve your prompt creation and make your video projects easier and faster.

The tool is designed for anyone, even if you don’t have experience in programming or video creation software. Just follow the instructions, and you will be able to create great prompts for your videos.

## ▶️ How to Use seedance2-skill

1. **Open the Application**  
   Launch seedance2-skill on your computer.

2. **Create a New Prompt**  
   Click the "New Prompt" button to start writing. You will see a simple text box.

3. **Write Your Video Idea**  
   Describe the video you want to generate using clear and specific language. For example:  
   *“Create a city skyline at sunset with flying cars and neon signs.”*

4. **Use Suggestions**  
   The app provides tips on improving your prompt. Follow these suggestions to add details like colors, actions, or mood.

5. **Save Your Prompt**  
   When done, save your prompt for later use by clicking the "Save" button.

6. **Export or Use with seedance2.0**  
   You can export your prompt as a text file or copy it to use in the seedance2.0 video generator.
